COLUMN DESCRIPTIONS OF RESTAURANT DATASET

 - id:  unique id of the restaurant being studied
 - visit_date: date being recorded
 - visitors:  number of visitors that showed up during that day
 - calendar_date:  calendar date for that day.  Either this or visit_date can be discarded
 - day_of_week:  what day of the week it is
 - holiday: whether or not that day was a japanese holiday
 - genre:  the genre of restaurant
 - area:  area of japan the restaurant was located in
 - latitude:  the latitude coordinate of the restaurant
 - longitude:  the longitude coordinate of the restaurant
 - reserve_visitors:  how many reservations the restaurant had via its online booking system
